## BATTLE MECHANICS

### Q16
- **Category**: Battle
- **Difficulty**: Easy
- **Question**: What does the Basic Attack do to NRG?
- **Options**: A) Costs 10 NRG  B) Has no effect  C) Restores 8 NRG  D) Drains all NRG
- **Answer**: C) Restores 8 NRG
- **Explanation**: Basic Attack costs 0 NRG and actually restores +8 NRG, making it a way to recharge for more powerful Special moves.

### Q17
- **Category**: Battle
- **Difficulty**: Easy
- **Question**: When does a switch action resolve in a turn?
- **Options**: A) After all moves  B) Based on Speed  C) Before any moves  D) Randomly
- **Answer**: C) Before any moves
- **Explanation**: Switches resolve first, before any moves. If either side switches, no moves resolve that turn.

### Q18
- **Category**: Battle
- **Difficulty**: Easy
- **Question**: What is the critical hit multiplier?
- **Options**: A) 1.2×  B) 1.5×  C) 2×  D) 1.3×
- **Answer**: B) 1.5×
- **Explanation**: Critical hits deal 1.5× normal damage and occur on a 10% chance per move.

### Q19
- **Category**: Battle
- **Difficulty**: Medium
- **Question**: What damage multiplier do Special moves use instead of ATK?
- **Options**: A) Base NRG stat  B) Current NRG  C) SPD stat  D) DEF stat
- **Answer**: B) Current NRG
- **Explanation**: Special moves use the attacker's CURRENT NRG (not base NRG stat). They get weaker as NRG is spent during battle.

### Q20
- **Category**: Battle
- **Difficulty**: Medium
- **Question**: What is the minimum fraction of damage that gets through Physical defense?
- **Options**: A) 10%  B) 15%  C) 20%  D) 5%
- **Answer**: C) 20%
- **Explanation**: The Physical defense formula is max(0.2, 1 − DEF/200), so at least 20% of damage always gets through, no matter how high DEF is.

### Q21
- **Category**: Battle
- **Difficulty**: Medium
- **Question**: What happens if both team members faint at the same time?
- **Options**: A) Attacker wins  B) Defender wins  C) Draw  D) The faster one wins
- **Answer**: C) Draw
- **Explanation**: If both teams are simultaneously fully defeated, the result is a draw. Neither side gains or loses Arena points.

### Q22
- **Category**: Battle
- **Difficulty**: Medium
- **Question**: What is the damage variance range for all moves?
- **Options**: A) 0.80–1.00  B) 0.75–1.25  C) 0.85–1.00  D) 0.90–1.10
- **Answer**: C) 0.85–1.00
- **Explanation**: Damage variance is calculated as 0.85 + random() × 0.15, giving a range of 0.85 to 1.00 (no upward variance above base).

### Q23
- **Category**: Battle
- **Difficulty**: Hard
- **Question**: Confuse status has a 30% chance to make a Berryvolution hurt itself. How much HP does the self-hit deal?
- **Options**: A) 5% maxHP  B) 10% maxHP  C) 15% maxHP  D) 20% maxHP
- **Answer**: B) 10% maxHP
- **Explanation**: When Confuse triggers self-damage, the attacker deals 10% of their own maxHP to themselves instead of executing their intended move.

### Q24
- **Category**: Battle
- **Difficulty**: Hard
- **Question**: What is the damage formula for a Physical move?
- **Options**: A) power × ATK × typeMult  B) (power/50) × ATK × typeMult × variance × max(0.2, 1−DEF/200)  C) power × ATK / DEF × typeMult  D) (power/100) × ATK × typeMult
- **Answer**: B) (power/50) × ATK × typeMult × variance × max(0.2, 1−DEF/200)
- **Explanation**: The full Physical damage formula accounts for power scaling, attacker's ATK, type effectiveness, variance, and a defense modifier with a 20% floor.
